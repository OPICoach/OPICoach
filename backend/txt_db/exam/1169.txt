Question 
How is the music you enjoy now different from the music you enjoyed in the past? Tell me about the changes in your taste in music.

Answer 
This question requires comparing past and present music tastes, using both past and present tenses. Answers should describe the changes in taste over time.

Feedback 
For Difficulty 5, prepare answers comparing past and present for various survey topics. Pay close attention to accurate tense usage and use varied verbs. Practice listening to longer questions as they appear at this level.
