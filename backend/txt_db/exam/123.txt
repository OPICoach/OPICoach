Question 
I would like to know where you live. Can you describe your home to me? What does it look like? Blah blah blah. How many rooms does it have? Give me a description with lots of details.

Answer 
Oh, you'd like to know about my place.

Feedback 
This is a beautiful and natural way to start. It's a rhetorical question that doesn't require an answer, making the conversation flow naturally. It's a very conversational way of asking a question.
