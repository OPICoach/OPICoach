Global Warming (Introduction)

Answer 
Global warming is a number one issue. The earth is undergoing global warming. And problems like global warming that are already upon us. And cause more forest death and more global warming. And we are doing that. So what we do, our choices we make can lead us towards global health or global sickness.

Feedback 
'upon us' means we are facing the problem. 'global health or global sickness' is a metaphorical expression for the Earth's condition.
