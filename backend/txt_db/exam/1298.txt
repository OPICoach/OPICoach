Question 
Have there been any changes to the public transportation system in your city since you were young?

Answer 
I think there have been a lot of changes in public transportation. I mean ages and ages ago there were not many subway lines and in the past there was no bus only lanes. but now everything is so different. There are many subway lines and I can go anywhere I want to. oh and another big change is the bus only lanes. We now have bus only lanes so there is less traffic jam. I personally think that this is the biggest change in public transportation system.
