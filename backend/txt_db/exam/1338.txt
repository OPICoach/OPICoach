Question 
I'd like to find out more information about the geography of your country. Could you please describe the mountains, rivers, seas, and others? Please tell me in detail.

Answer 
The country I'm living in is Korea. located in East Asia right between China and Japan. It's a peninsula surrounded by oceans on three sides. 70% covered with mountains and per mountain, there are so many trees. Oh and there is a river. It's really famous. It's called the Han River. The river runs through the city. And there are 12 different Han River parks along the Han River. I love visiting different places in Korea.
