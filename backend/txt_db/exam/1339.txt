Question 
Is there any geographically special or unique area in your neighborhood? Why is it unique and what's so special about it? Please tell me in detail.

Answer 
We have a special river called the Han River in our neighborhood. First of all, This river runs through Seoul, the capital city of Korea. And there are 12 different Han River parks along the Han River. So people have easy access to parks. The atmosphere is always active and lively. Han River Parks offer breathtaking scenery and fresh air. So when I admire the view, it feels amazing. I would like to recommend this place to all tourists visiting Korea.
