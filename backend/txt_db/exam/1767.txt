Question 
Do you have any experience of going on a trip to another country? How is the geography there different from your own country?

Answer 
도시에 대해서 비교를 하고 싶어요. 일본이랑 한국 이 두 나라에 대해서 비교를 해 보겠습니다. (Japan) 일본은 낮은 빌딩들이 많았다. (Korea) 반대로 높은 빌딩들이 많았다.

Feedback 
This is a non-time comparison question (A vs B). Choosing to compare cities/buildings rather than natural landscapes is a valid and often easier approach. The main point clearly states the comparison (Japan vs Korea, cities/geography). Using opposite descriptions like 'low buildings' (or 'not tall buildings') and 'tall buildings' is a very effective way to highlight the differences for the rater.
