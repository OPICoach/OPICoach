Question 
Describe something you bought that was expensive.

Answer 
The book was expensive. Seriously, it was really expensive. It was a limited edition.

Feedback 
This answer follows the S-R-R strategy. 'The book was expensive' is the simple statement. 'Seriously, it was really expensive' repeats the feeling using 'Seriously' and 'really'. 'It was a limited edition' gives the reason. This structure adds detail to the simple statement.
