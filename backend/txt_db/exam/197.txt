Question 
당신이 만들기 좋아하는 요리는 무엇인가요? 어떻게 만드시나요? 자세히 설명해 주세요.

Answer 
I like to cook Kimchi fried rice. It's a fried rice made of Kimchi. Kimchi is a traditional Korean food. To start, I first need to chop all the ingredients such as Kimchi and pork. Then I fry them with rice and some oil. Lastly, I put a fried egg on top. Then it's done. I cook it often because it's very easy to make.
