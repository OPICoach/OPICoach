Question 
How did you become interested in [cooking]? 몇 살이니 그리고 누가 누가 알려 주셨나요? Who did you make your first meal for?

Answer 
I first became interested in cooking when I was eight years old. I wanted to do something for my mother's birthday. So I decided to cook seaweed soup. Seaweed soup is a traditional Korean food we eat on birthday. I love seaweed soup because it's full of nutrients. My father and I searched online, printed out a recipe. He helped me get the ingredients and taught me how to cook. It was very delicious. My mother was very happy and touched. To this day, she tells me she was very happy I made it for her.
