Question 
I'd like to give you a situation to act out. Suppose that you want to go shopping with your friend. Call your friend and ask three or four questions about shopping together.

Answer 
Hi [Friend's Name], do you want to go shopping this weekend? Which mall should we go to? What time should we meet? Are you looking for anything specific?

Feedback 
'Hi [Friend's Name], do you want to go shopping this weekend?' is a direct invitation. 'Which mall should we go to?' asks about the location. 'What time should we meet?' asks about the timing. 'Are you looking for anything specific?' helps understand their shopping goals. Natural questions for planning a shopping trip with a friend.
