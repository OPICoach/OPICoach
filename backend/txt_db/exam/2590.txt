Question 
You indicated that you like listening to music in the survey. What types of music do you enjoy listening to? And tell me about your favorite music.

Answer 
I like two different types of music, dance and classical. When I feel depressed and unhappy, I listen to dance music, which makes me refreshed and energetic. On the other hand, I listen to classical music when I'm tired or suffer from insomnia. The gentle sound of the classical music helps me relax and sleep tight. It also clears my mind, so that I can focus on what I do and what I can do.
