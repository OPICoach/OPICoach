Question 
If my answers to different questions contradict each other (e.g., saying 'I'm an only child' in one answer and 'I have a sister' in another), will I be penalized?

Answer 
No, there is no penalty for contradictory answers.

Feedback 
OPIc is a test that measures only your English proficiency, not factual accuracy or consistency between your answers. The raters focus solely on your language skills.
