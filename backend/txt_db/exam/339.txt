Question 
What kind of activities do children do at parks? How are they different from what adults do? Please compare the activities that children do with the ones that adults do at parks. Please give me all the details.

Answer 
Well, I think adults usually exercise, relax on the grass, enjoy the scenery, or have picnics with family and friends. Children do some of these activities as well. However, children mostly go to parks to hang out with their family or friends. I don't think children go to parks by themselves to unwind or blow off some steam. Whenever I go to a park, I see many children hanging out or riding bicycles with their friends or family. So the point is, children never go to parks alone to get some rest. Also, in my perspective, children aren't that serious about enjoying scenery at parks. I think kids mostly go to parks to play with their friends.
