Question 
When you lack general experience related to a topic or activity.

Answer 
I don't have a lot of experience with that. / I don't have a lot of experience with [noun]. / I don't have a lot of experience [verb-ing].

Feedback 
This is a useful phrase to state a general lack of experience. It helps fill silence and signals why you might find the question difficult. You can specify the area of lack of experience using "with + noun" or "with + gerund".
