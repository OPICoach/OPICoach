Question 
Where and when do you usually listen to music? What do you use to listen to music?

Answer 
Without music, life would be a mistake. Seoul Forest is the best place to enjoy my leisure time. On weekends, I listen to the music at the park. My workout music tracks can boost my physical performance and mood in a short time. Obviously, playing soft music in the background helps me stay focused. My music streaming services give me access to various songs. To be happy in this world, first I need a cell phone. It fits easily into my pocket. It doesn't weigh much. There are so many things I can do with my cell phone. 'Be the best place to' 패턴은 다른 주제에도 활용 가능하니 기억해두세요.
