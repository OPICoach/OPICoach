Question 
What is your favorite song? Do you have a special memory associated any songs with the songs?

Answer 
I'm a music lover. One of my favorite songs is Dynamite by BTS. Card on the table, I truly like the lyrics in the song. This song makes me strive for my goal with a full of energy. Whenever I hear that song, I get nostalgic. There is a reason why music can trigger forgotten memories. My childhood Christmas experiences were filled with happiness and family gatherings. When I was 11, I heard a disco pop for the first time. Anyway, this is my favorite song and memorable experience.
