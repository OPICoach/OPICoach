Question 
어떠한 콘서트홀을 당신은 자주 가시나요? 그곳은 어떻게 생겼나요? 왜 그곳에 가는 것을 좋아하나요? (What concert hall do you often go to? What does it look like? Why do you like to go there?)

Answer 
My favorite concert hall is the Olympic Stadium. It's the biggest concert hall in Korea. I love this place because it's really close to my house. And it opens 24/7, right? And it has a good sound system. And a big screen. Oh and staff members are kind. I always have a great time whenever I go there.
