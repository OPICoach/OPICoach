Question 
How did you develop your interest in bike riding? Who taught how to ride a bike?

Answer 
I started riding a bike when I was seven. My father taught me how to ride a bike when I was in an elementary school. He was too busy to spend time with me on weekdays. On weekends, we used to ride a bike together at a park. Nowadays, it is a gorgeous way to commute every morning without traffic jams. I have so much fun riding my bike. To sum up, that's all about my experience.
