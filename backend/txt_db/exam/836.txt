Question 
What is your basic routine like when you go hiking? What do you usually do before you start hiking? What do you do while you are on a hike? Please tell me in as much detail as possible.

Answer 
I usually go hiking on weekends to get some exercise. You know, I sometimes invite some friends to go hiking with me because hiking or trekking is a great outdoor activity that I can do with my friends. Whenever I go hiking, I never forget to do some warm up exercise to avoid injury. Also, when I hike by myself, I normally listen to some music. Hiking alone would be really boring without any music. You know, I often go to a mountain near my house. When I reach the top, I stay there for about 10 or 20 minutes to enjoy the beautiful scenery. Well, I usually take some pictures of myself and the scenery. I sometimes take some selfies. Especially in the spring, this place is beautiful with lots of flowers. Actually, spring is the most beautiful time of year in Korea. Even though hiking is an easy way to exercise, I usually feel very hungry after hiking. As a matter of fact, there are a lot of traditional Korean restaurant near the starting point of my hiking path. When I'm with my friends, I always visit one of the restaurants and have some Korean style barbecue and bibimbap which is a mixture of rice, fresh veggies and some egg and Korean traditional hot sauce. It's so tasty and also good for health. You know, it's pretty nutritious. So, these are the things that I usually do when I go hiking.
