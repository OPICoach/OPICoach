Question 
Has anything memorable or special ever happened while you are on a hike? Where were you at? Who were you with? Provide me as many details as possible.

Answer 
When I was about 13, my family took a trip to Island. Island, you know, it has very unique geography because it's a volcanic island. On the second day of the trip, my family went hiking to Mount Halla. which is the biggest mountain in South Korea. On that day the weather was really hot and humid. Besides, the mountain was steeper than I expected. It was pretty tough to reach the top. My dad and I didn't say a word. We just walked and walked. I didn't understand why my dad had been so excited about this at first. However, when we reached the top, I was surprised by the breathtaking scenery. I couldn't believe my eyes. It was just picturesque. I still can't forget the scenery and the fresh air. The memory at the top of the mountain is unforgettable. I will never forget the beautiful scenery. I don't think I can express how impressive the beautiful scenery was. Also, I was so proud of myself for not giving up. I think it's one of the best trips I've ever taken.
