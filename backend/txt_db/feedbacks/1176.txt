Question 
Seasons, weather in your country. 너희 나라의 계절, 날씨를 묘사해 봐.

Answer 
Korea has four distinct seasons, each with totally different weather. During the summer, it's incredibly hot in Korea. It gets quite humid too. The winter in Korea is kind of long and dry. In the winter, it gets super cold. I definitely prefer the spring because I literally hate the cold.
