Question 
Do you have a responsibility to do recycling in your house? If so, please tell me how you do recycling in detail.

Answer 
Yes, Every Sunday, I do recycling with my family members. And each of us has different responsibilities. My father collects all the recyclable items, such as glass, plastics, and paper. Then, My brother and I assort them to the right back. We make sure not to mix some things. When we are done with sorting out, we take them out to a recycling site and put them in the right places. My mother is responsible for monitoring. I think recycling is important to go green.
