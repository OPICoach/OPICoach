Question 
OPIc Role-playing: Handling a Broken Appointment (Common in Q12)

Answer 
Key expressions taught: I will not be able to make it to [event/place], I will totally make it up to you, You can call me anytime and I'll get right back to you, Text me anytime and I'll text you, You can call me on my cellphone, Let me know if anything happens, A thousand apologies, I'll buy you a cup of coffee.

Feedback 
These expressions are essential for the frequently tested broken appointment scenario in OPIc role-playing. They are sourced from natural English usage and help demonstrate fluency and naturalness required for higher OPIc grades like IH and AL. Practice shadowing these expressions for effective use in the exam.
