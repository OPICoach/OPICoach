Question 
Tell me about cafes or coffee shops in your country. What are they like? What do people usually do there?

Answer 
Cafes are extremely popular in Korea; they are everywhere! There's a huge variety, from large international chains to small, cozy independent shops. They are usually very well-decorated and comfortable. People go there for many reasons: to study, work, meet friends, have business meetings, or just relax and enjoy a drink. Many students use cafes as study spots.
