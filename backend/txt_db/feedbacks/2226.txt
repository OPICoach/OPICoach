Question 
쇼핑과 관련된 기억에 남는 경험에 대해 이야기해 주세요. (쇼핑 문제 중 마지막 고난도 문제)

Answer 
Just three days ago, I bought a new laptop for my mom as her birthday present. But the laptop I bought was defective because the laptop didn't work properly and made some weird noise. So I brought them back to the shop and asked one of the staff there to give me a refund. However, he said it was not available because of their company rule. So I asked them whether I can exchange it to another one. They allowed me to do. So eventually, I bought another laptop for my mom's present. I hope this will never happen again.

Feedback 
발음, 발화량 등은 우수합니다. 시제 오류는 감지되지 않았으며, 'brought them back' 또는 'allowed me to do'와 같은 단어 선정(word choice)에서의 미세한 오류는 AL 등급에서 크게 문제 삼지 않습니다. 시제 오류가 없는 점이 IH 답변과의 차이점입니다.
