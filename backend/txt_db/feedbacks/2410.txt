Question 
I'd like to give you a situation and ask you to act it out. You need to rent a car for about a week. Call the rental agency and ask several questions about the car you want to rent.

Answer 
Hi, I need to rent a car for about a week, starting [Date]. What types of cars are available? How much does it cost per day or per week? Is insurance included in the price?

Feedback 
'I need to rent a car for about a week...' is a clear statement of purpose. 'What types of cars are available?' is a necessary question. 'How much does it cost...?' is essential for budgeting. 'Is insurance included...?' is a very important question when renting a car. Excellent questions.
