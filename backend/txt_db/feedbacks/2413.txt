Question 
I'd like to give you a situation and ask you to act it out. You want to join a gym. Call them and ask three or four questions to get all the information you need.

Answer 
Hello, I'm interested in joining your gym. What are your membership fees? What are your operating hours? Do you offer any fitness classes?

Feedback 
'I'm interested in joining your gym' is a good way to start. 'What are your membership fees?' is essential information. 'What are your operating hours?' is also very important for planning visits. 'Do you offer any fitness classes?' is a common question for gyms. Clear and relevant questions.
