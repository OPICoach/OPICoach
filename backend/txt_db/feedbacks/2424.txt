Question 
I would like to visit your country. You would like to help me. Ask me three or four questions about my interests to find out more about me so that you can help me better with my trip to your country.

Answer 
That's great you want to visit Korea! What kind of things do you like to do when you travel? Are you interested in history and culture, or more in nature and outdoor activities? What kind of food do you enjoy?

Feedback 
'That's great you want to visit Korea!' shows enthusiasm. 'What kind of things do you like to do when you travel?' is a broad question to start. 'Are you interested in history and culture, or more in nature and outdoor activities?' asks about specific interests. 'What kind of food do you enjoy?' is important for recommendations. Excellent questions to tailor recommendations.
