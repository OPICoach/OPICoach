Question 
What are the three main reasons why IH students don't get AL?

Answer 
There are three big reasons. They lack salt, pepper, and kimchi. It's like eating bland gomtang without salt, pepper, or kimchi. Their answers are bland to the grader. AL students don't do it blandly because they have salt, pepper, and kimchi.

Feedback 
Salt represents contrast. Pepper represents repeating. Kimchi represents advanced filler. These strategies were developed by studying 30 IH students to find the reasons they were stuck at IH.
